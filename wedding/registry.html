    Preferred Charities

    Gifts are not necessary or required - the best gift you can give us is travelling out to celebrate with us. If you do want to give, we're asking that in lieu of gifts you donate to one of our favorite charitable organizations.

    Fare Start

    • Fare Start is a non-profit organization in Seattle that provides job training in the food industry to at-risk youth and adults living below the poverty line. Adult students participate in four months of culinary training while receiving counseling and other life skills support to ensure their success outside of the program. Students under the age of 24 who are without stable housing or still in school undergo 8 weeks of barista or culinary training to give them the skills necessary to begin their careers.

    Blue Sky Bakery

    • Blue Sky Bakery provides culinary training to at risk youth over a period of three months and provides support once the program is over to help place them in jobs. This training and job placement helps youth find jobs while dealing with unstable housing, criminal records, and other issues.

    Local First Arizona

    • Local First Arizona (LFA) is a statewide non-profit organization working to strengthen communities and local economies through growing, supporting, and celebrating locally owned businesses throughout the state of Arizona. They educate consumers, stakeholders, business leaders, and policymakers about the significant economic, civic, and cultural benefits of strong local economies.
